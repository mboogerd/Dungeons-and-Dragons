Excerpt from "Vessels of Power" 
- An ancient text, the rest of which is lost.
"… was the Crown, which gifted us the potential of transformation until jealousy stole its might from it. As the crown was shattered, it cried but most its power was dispersed into the four winds. Unaware of the one Witness of their secret, the thief drank up as many of the tears until they could drink no more, intending to leave only echoes in the sacred spaces of the world. But for those who know where to look, the Shattered Crown can still be found."

“The final stroke came from the light itself, for even wisdom may be made blind. A keeper of fate wove a new story, and so the fire was left to be unwritten.”

“Once there was but a single crown, and it burned as bright as the dawn. None ruled above them, and the Courts bowed in harmony.”

“From the ruins rose a queen, crowned in summer’s gold. She called the Courts to order, binding them in law, and none questioned why the fire no longer burned.” 

“The second blade fell with the weight of forgotten names. A scholar of stars carved a path through memory, and so the fire was left to be forgotten.” 

“When the spiral is drawn in ash and four flames burn as one, the crownless shall rise again. And the lie shall turn to cinders.”

“And so the flame was shattered in the heart of the forest, where no path leads and no memory lingers. What was whole became many, what was many became none.” 

“But the embers cannot be unmade, only scattered. And where one ember lands, another will rise.” 

“The first seed of treachery was sown in the roots of winter. A great tree was convinced that the fire would consume all, and so the fire was left to freeze.” 

“But the golden hand of ambition moves unseen, and whispers weave sharper blades than steel. One among them longed for a throne of her own.”

“Now, the world shudders in quiet unrest. The rivers run warm where they should freeze, and the wind carries whispers of a name no tongue remembers.”
