# Dungeons and Dragons

This site publishes the Obsidian vault as a browsable reference for campaigns, session notes, lore, people, locations, factions, items, and images.

The most effective ways to navigate it are the folder explorer, search, and backlinks.

## Start Here

- [Enter the Dungeon](./Enter%20the%20Dungeon/): the most developed published campaign folder at the moment, centered on CSI, [[Neonetheril]], the [[Sluitsteen]], [[Chardalyn]], and the current [[Cormyr]] / [[Suzail]] espionage arc.
- [Enter the Dungeon sessions overview](./Enter%20the%20Dungeon/Sessions/Sessions): the best top-level summary of that campaign's structure and current state.
