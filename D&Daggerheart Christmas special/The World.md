Netheril has been the pinnacle of arcane achievement for centuries, most clearly expressed in their flying cities held up in the sky by the weave itself. Its citizens enjoy abundance and luxury—that is, the inhabitants of the skycities do. But not all Netherese live in the sky. Most (roughly 80%) spend their lives on the ground in simple rural communities, far from the high society centers of power, magic, and wealth. While up there it seems like time stands still, down on the ground more and more signs of ecological collapse are appearing. Harvests grow smaller each year, wildlife becomes increasingly scarce, and wildfires, droughts, and even earth tremors occur with alarming frequency. In this Christmas Special, you'll play as young adults coming of age in a rapidly changing world. Who will you become and what role will you play? Whether you've always lived on the ground or have accidentally or intentionally found your way down from one of the sky cities is up to you, but soon none of you will be able to ignore the price the land is paying for Netheril's arcane might.
