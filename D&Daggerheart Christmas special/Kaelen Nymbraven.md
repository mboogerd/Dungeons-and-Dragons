Name: Kaelen Nymbraven
Class / Subclass: Bard / Wordsmith
Ancestry: Halfling / Faerie
Community: Highborne

# Background
Kaelen was born in Karathul, a skycity ruled by the magocratic elite, the House of Nymbraven among them. His father, a halfling noble, raised him alone after his moth-like faerie mother returned only occasionally via the Feywild. Educated in rhetoric, history, and the subtleties of Loross, Kaelen grew up among the gilded towers but never lost sight of the city’s darker underbelly. He began his career early, following in his father's footsteps. Officially, House Nymbraven is in charge of the Karathul Archives. Practically, it is the city’s public relations department, and he was quickly spinning favorable narratives for the noble Houses. Over time, however, he uncovered systemic corruption, ecological collapse caused by magical overreach, and abuses of the groundling population. A key turning point came with the Frost-Garden Collapse, and the death of Elidran Marrast — a colleague — whose hidden notes Kaelen discovered. Later, the Starfall Papers and the murder of his former tutor Celyra Valxior cemented his break with the city’s elite.
Role in the Community
- Former role: PR writer for the Magocracy, crafting narratives for the ruling Houses.
- Current role: Whistleblower, journalist, and underground information broker.
- Network: Trusted contacts among sympathetic guilds, printers, and former city scribes (e.g., Jevan Tollspire, Serenya Vellorin, Taviss Qirel). Uses trade papers like The Guildsman’s Eye to anonymously expose corruption.
- Daily life: Lives in rotating, modest lodgings on the ground; survives via bardic performances, ghostwriting, and discreet trading of rare items. Constantly moves to avoid detection.

# Character
- Personality: Cautious, clever, and morally driven; motivated by a sense of justice and personal loyalty to those who trusted him. Witty and eloquent, with a subtle bardic flair.
- Appearance & Traits: Small stature (halfling heritage), expressive features, moth-like grace, eyes and wings inherited from his mother. Keeps a discreet signet pendant of House Nymbraven for emergencies. Always carries modular disguises for rapid identity changes.
- Habits: Reads and writes obsessively; cherishes a small hidden collection of luxury items from the skycity; haunted by past tragedies (Frost-Garden victims, Celyra Valxior).

# Story Seed
Kaelen is now a fugitive journalist, uncovering injustices in the shadow of Karathul, motivated by his parent's principles, personal tragedy and a sense of impending doom. Whether investigating magical experiments that endanger the ground, tracking the flow of corrupt guild contracts, or exposing clandestine abuses by noble Houses, he walks a fine line between anonymity and impact. Each article risks exposure, but every secret he reveals builds the case against the city’s elite — and draws him ever closer to enemies who would see him silenced forever.
Experiences- The Truth Has Many Faces. Always carries equipment to quickly establish a new identity, create a distraction or anything else that allows him to make a discreet exit.- Tell Me More. Is adept at putting people at ease and making them divulge more than they realize.
