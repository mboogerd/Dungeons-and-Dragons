
## 🌱 Ecological & Sustaining Roles
•	**Mycelial Tenders** (Gardeners)
Nurture the vast fungal networks and gardens, ensuring the flow of nutrients. They cultivate spores, tend the soil, and heal diseased patches of mycelium.
*Interactions*: Provide sustenance for Healers, Lorekeepers, and the whole community.
•	**Spore Nurturers** (Midwives)
Oversee the creation and growth of new Fungril, guiding sporelings through their early development.
*Interactions*: Supported by Lorekeepers (passing on ancestral memory) and Healers (ensuring safe growth).
•	**Symbionts** (Animal & Plant Stewards)
Maintain relationships with plants and animals—guiding pollinators, feeding symbiotic animals, and balancing the ecosystem.
*Interactions*: Collaborate with Mycelial Tenders for plant growth and with Hunters/Explorers for **balance**.
⸻
## 🌿 Knowledge & Guidance Roles
•	**Lorekeepers** (Memory Holders): Preserve ancestral wisdom, stories, and collective memory (potentially “recorded” in the mycelial network).
*Interactions*: Advise Leaders and Educators; transmit cultural cohesion.
•	**Healers & Alchemists**: Use fungi’s medicinal and alchemical properties to cure disease, brew tonics, and ward off corruption.
*Interactions*: Work closely with Mycelial Tenders to source materials; collaborate with Spore Nurturers for healthy growth.
•	**Educators / Mentors**: Guide sporelings and younger Fungril, teaching them roles, ethics, and practical skills.
*Interactions*: Act as bridges between Lorekeepers, Leaders, and Spore Nurturers.
⸻
## 🍄 Governance & Community Roles

•	**Council of Hyphae** (Decision-Makers): A decentralized leadership model, where each representative speaks for part of the community (like nodes in a network).
*Interactions*: Draw upon Lorekeepers’ wisdom, Scouts’ findings, and the needs voiced by each role.
•	**Ritualists / Spiritual Guides**: Maintain the community’s connection to cycles, seasons, and perhaps the magical Chardalyn. Lead ceremonies of renewal, healing, and mourning.
*Interactions*: Support all roles, acting as unifiers in times of stress or celebration.
⸻
## 🌌 Exploration & Defense Roles

•	**Scouts / Pathfinders**: Venture into new lands to seek nutrients, resources, or safe habitats.
*Interactions*: Report findings to Council; provide opportunities for Gardeners and Stewards.
•	**Hunters / Wardens**: Defend the community from threats (animal predators, rival groups, magical corruption). May also hunt in a balanced, ritualized way.
*Interactions*: Coordinate with Healers for treatment and with Stewards to maintain ecological balance.
•	**Purifiers / Corruption-Binders**: Specialized Fungril trained to identify and cleanse corrupted mycelium or Chardalyn.
*Interactions*: Rely on Healers for remedies and Ritualists for support.
⸻
## 🌟 Interactions (The Web of Society)

•	Cyclical Collaboration: Spore Nurturers grow new Fungril → Educators and Lorekeepers shape them → Gardeners and Stewards sustain them → Healers and Ritualists protect them → Council guides their path.
•	Decentralized Leadership: No single “ruler”; instead, decisions flow like nutrients through the mycelial web—slow, distributed, but resilient.
•	Resilience Through Specialization: Each role has unique expertise, but survival depends on collaboration, not isolation.
