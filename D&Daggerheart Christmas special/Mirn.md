Name: Mirn
Class / Subclass: Warrior — Path of the Brave
Ancestry: Giant
Community: Wanderborne

**Background**:
Mirn grew up in a wandering nomadic clan that followed the herds and rivers, moving whenever the land could no longer sustain them. His life has been one of hardship and endurance: cold winters, empty stomachs, and the endless rhythm of building and breaking camp.

**Role in the Clan**:
Among his people, Mirn is a warrior. His hammer was first the tool that drove the great tent-poles into frozen ground, but in his hands it has become a weapon as well. He fights with the same blunt strength he once used to build homes.

**Character**:
Mirn is quiet and obedient, shaped by the fatalism of his elders. He trusts in the lessons of the clan: live with the land, endure what it gives, and wait for the world to heal in its own time. He has never questioned those voices — he has always followed them. Yet beneath that obedience, another truth has been growing in him. The skies above are filled with the floating cities of the mages, draining the land until nothing remains. The elders never told him to hate them — but how could he not, when their shadow falls across every dying river, every barren field, every broken herd?

**Story Seed**:
This is the story of a boy who has only ever been a follower, echoing the truths of others. In time, he will face the moment when the voices of his elders fall silent — and he must discover what it means to speak with a voice of his own.
