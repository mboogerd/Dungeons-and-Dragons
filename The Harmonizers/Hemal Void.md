## Introduction
In the intricate tapestry of your Dungeons & Dragons campaign emerges a character of profound complexity and enigmatic allure: Hemal Void. Hemal is not merely a character; he is an embodiment of a concept – the embodiment of balance within the universe. Created as a warforged being, Hemal’s existence is intertwined with advanced magitech, making him a unique blend of arcane magic and sophisticated technology.

At first glance, Hemal's imposing presence is unmissable. A warforged humanoid, his body is a striking juxtaposition of two differently colored metals, symbolizing the dual nature of his existence and purpose. The left side, adorned in nearly black metal, contrasts sharply with the right side's white-silver sheen. His eyes mirror this contrast – one white, the other deep black. His skin, visible beneath the armor, is etched with red lines that whisper of both life and circuitry, converging at a pulsating red crystal embedded in his chest. This crystal is not just for show; it is an Ethereal Nanocircuit connected to a Nexus Crystal, a rare artifact born from the Nexus at the heart of the Citadel – a central hub in the universe channeling the ethereal aether.

Hemal’s personality is as layered as his physical form. Calm, polite, and logical, he often seems detached, lost in the whispers of spirits visible only to him, a consequence of his unique connection to the Nexus and its life-capturing crystals. His youth belies a deep-seated understanding of and dedication to his core principle: Balance. To Hemal, balance is not just an ideal; it is the very essence of his being, the mandate that guides every action he takes, every decision he makes.

Equipped with a spear that is an extension of his own body, armor that is as much a part of him as his own artificial skin, and soul cages that carry more than just power, Hemal is a formidable figure on any battlefield. His combat style, a reflection of his mission, is about maintaining balance – sometimes ruthlessly so. His magical abilities, categorized by their color themes, are not just attacks or defenses; they are expressions of his very purpose.

In Hemal Void, we find a character who is more than a mere participant in your campaign. He is a question posed to every encounter, every conflict – a question about the nature of power, the price of balance, and the complexity of choice in a universe teeming with magic, technology, and the unknown.

### Physical Description and Visual Elements

Hemal Void, a character of striking appearance and intricate design, stands as a testament to the seamless integration of magic and technology in your D&D universe. His physical form is not just a body; it's a canvas displaying the marvels of arcane engineering.

**Bodily Construction**: Hemal's body is a harmonious blend of two distinctly colored metals. The left side of his frame is sheathed in a nearly black metal, exuding an aura of mystery and strength. In contrast, his right side gleams with the lustrous sheen of white-silver metal, symbolizing purity and precision. This dichotomy is not merely cosmetic; it reflects the dual nature of his existence and purpose.

**Eyes and Facial Features**: Perhaps the most striking features are his eyes – one pure white and the other deep black, mirroring the duality of his metallic body. His face, though warforged and mechanical, carries an expression of calm intelligence and purpose. The skin, visible beneath the segmented armor, is a neutral gray, laced with intricate red lines that resemble both veins and electronic circuits, hinting at the complex life force and technological prowess within.

**Chest Crystal and Circuits**: At the center of his chest lies the Nexus Crystal, a pulsating red gem connected to ethereal nanocircuits. These circuits are not merely decorative; they are a visual representation of Hemal's magical and life energies. The intensity and behavior of the light within the crystal and the circuits vary, indicating his current magical capacity and the flow of energy during spellcasting.

**Arm Equipment**: Hemal's arms are distinct in their own right. His left lower arm houses three red blood orbs, soul cages that appear empty or filled with a whispy red cloud, depending on their current state. His right arm, mechanically more intricate, harbors a retractable spear – a weapon that is as much a part of him as any limb, ready to materialize at his command.

**Armor Integration**: Overlying his artificial skin is armor that provides not just protection but also serves as an interface for his magical abilities. Black tendrils connect the armor to his body, symbolizing the deep integration of his defensive capabilities with his very being.

**Spellcasting Visuals**: Hemal's spellcasting is a spectacle of color-coordinated magic. Red spells pulse along his circuits, black and white spells manifest as tendrils lashing out from corresponding parts of his body, and grey spells add a subtler, more mysterious visual element. Each spell cast is not just an action but a display of his inner workings, with energy visibly coursing through him, reflecting the nature of the magic being wielded.
### Personality and Traits

Hemal Void, in his quest to uphold the cosmic balance, exhibits a personality and set of traits that are as unique as his physical form. These characteristics define not only his interactions but also provide a window into the soul of this warforged being.

**Calm and Composed**: Hemal's default demeanor is one of tranquility and politeness. He approaches situations with a serene composure, rarely showing signs of agitation or distress. This calmness is not just a personality trait but a manifestation of his warforged nature, designed to maintain focus and clarity in the midst of chaos.

**Logical and Reasoned**: Hemal thinks and acts with a logic-driven mindset. Decisions are made through careful analysis and reasoning, often reflecting his deep programming and understanding of balance. His approach to problems is methodical, prioritizing rationality over emotion.

**Youthful Inexperience**: Despite his advanced abilities and understanding of complex concepts like balance and power, Hemal's "youth" in terms of his time since creation or awakening means he is still navigating the nuances of existence. His interactions are marked by a certain naivety or lack of worldly wisdom, offering a contrast to his otherwise profound nature.

**Focused on the Ethereal**: Hemal often appears distracted or distant, but this is not a sign of disinterest. Instead, it is a reflection of his unique ability to perceive spirits and ethereal energies invisible to others. This trait sometimes distances him from those around him, as he engages with realities that others cannot sense.

**Devotion to Balance**: At the core of Hemal's being is an unwavering commitment to maintaining universal balance. He views this as his paramount duty, a principle that guides every action and decision. This dedication, though noble, often leads to a rigid interpretation of situations, viewing events through the lens of balance above all else.

**Serious and Uncompromising**: Hemal's seriousness is a direct result of his deep devotion to his cause. He seldom indulges in humor or frivolity, maintaining a grave demeanor, especially when discussing matters related to balance. His approach in combat is equally uncompromising, willing to make tough choices, including taking lives, if he deems it necessary for the greater equilibrium.

**Relentless Pursuit of Duty**: Hemal's bond with The Equalizers is driven by a shared commitment to his ideals. He views their survival and success as crucial to his mission, as long as they align with the greater goal of maintaining balance.

In essence, Hemal Void is a character of contrasts – serene yet serious, logical yet inexperienced, focused on an unseen world yet deeply committed to the physical one. His personality and traits make him not just a guardian of balance but also a figure of intrigue and complexity

### Magical Abilities and Spell Categories

Hemal Void's arsenal of magical abilities is as intricate as his physical and mental makeup. His spells are categorized into four distinct themes, each represented by a color that not only signifies the nature of the magic but also aligns with his unique warforged essence. This categorization not only simplifies the understanding of his abilities but also adds a layer of visual and thematic depth to his character.

**1. Red Spells (Lifeforce and Soul Manipulation)**

- **Core Theme**: These spells revolve around the manipulation of lifeforce and souls, particularly drawing from those who disrupt the balance of the universe. The energy harnessed is used to either harm adversaries or bolster Hemal and his allies.
- **Key Spells**:
    - _Soul Cage_: Captures souls for later use, aligning with the red orbs in his arm.
    - _Life Transference_: Utilizes his own lifeforce to heal others, often replenished by other red spells.
    - _Wither and Bloom_: Heals through the energy of nearby enemies.
    - _Vampiric Touch / Enervation_: Employs necrotic energy for attack and self-healing.
- **Visuals**: Activation of these spells is marked by the glowing of his central Nexus Crystal and the propagation of bright, blood-red pulses along his body's circuits.

**2. Black Spells (Pure Necrotic and Poison Damage)**

- **Core Theme**: Black spells focus on inflicting necrotic or poison damage directly, without the reciprocal effect of healing or energy transfer.
- **Key Spells**:
    - _Chill Touch_, _Infestation_, _Toll the Dead_: Direct damage spells.
    - _Arms of Hadar_, _Hex_: Enhances his attacks with dark energy.
- **Visuals**: These spells are visually represented by the activation of his left black eye and the black right side of his body, manifesting as dark tendrils reaching towards the target.

**3. White Spells (Soul and Psychic Targeting)**

- **Core Theme**: White spells target the soul and psyche, causing psychic damage or mental effects.
- **Key Spells**:
    - _Befuddle Creature_, _Friends_, _Mind Sliver_, _Wrathful Smite_, _Crown of Madness_, _Detect Thoughts_, _Mind Spike_, _Antagonize_, _Dominate Beast_, _Phantasmal Killer_, _Staggering Smite_, _Dominate Person_, _Synaptic Static_.
- **Visuals**: Manifest as white tendrils emitting from the white left side of his body and his right white eye, symbolizing the targeting of the soul and mind.

**4. Gray Spells (Miscellaneous and Utility)**

- **Core Theme**: Gray spells do not strictly fall into the other categories and often include utility or less thematic spells.
- **Key Spells**:
    - _Light_, _Message_, _Spare the Dying_, _See Invisibility_, _Animate Dead_, _Speak with Dead_.
- **Visuals**: These spells have more subtle and varied visual effects, reflecting their diverse nature and utility.

Hemal's magical abilities are not just tools for combat; they are extensions of his very being, each color-coded spell category resonating with a specific aspect of his warforged nature. This intricate spell system not only enhances his role as a guardian of balance but also provides a vivid and visually striking element to his presence in the campaign.

# The first encounter
Voor je verschijnt een warforged humanoid. Hij is grotendeels bepantserd met twee verschillende kleuren metaal. De linkerhelft van zijn lichaam is bekleed met bijna zwart metaal, terwijl zijn rechterzijde bekleed is met een wit-zilverkleurig metaal. In contrast daarmee staan zijn ogen, links volledig wit, en rechts diep zwart. De huid, als je het zo kan noemen, die her en der onder het pantser zichtbaar heeft een onbestemd grijs, maar is ingelegd met rode lijnen die je tegelijkertijd doen laten denken aan aderen en electronica borden. De lijnen omlijsten zijn gezicht en armen en komen samen in het midden van zijn borst, waar een groot rood kristal rustig pulseert. Zijn linkerarm is ingelegd met drie halve dieprode bollen, die in contrast met de andere rode lijnen, leeg aandoen. Zijn rechterarm is verder uitgebouwd dan de linker, met complexe mechanieken waarvan de functie niet in eens helder is.

Terwijl hij daar staat, vergezeld door een engineer, richt hij zich tot jullie, in een zacht melodische, comfortabele stem, ondanks duidelijk wat synthetische randjes:

"Allow me to introduce myself. I am Hemal Void. I have been specifically designed and forged to  complement the chosen members of The Equilizers. Installed within me is my mission to (intensity rising) protect _The Balance_. No force, collectively condemned evil or self-proclaimed good, should have a magnitude that dwarfs all others. All power must be accompanied with checks and balances!"

At this point, the accompanying engineer jumps in as he whispers; "he... he's a little intense at times, we're still finetuning".

Hemal continues undisturbed in a deep voice: "I _am_ this check"... He glares at you. "_We_ will _be_ this check!". And in crescendo: "For as long as our intentions align and my blood circuits pulsate, I will be your lifeline!"

...

Overdramatische introducties daargelaten, over de volgende vijf jaar leren jullie Hemal kennen als een sociaal vaardige warforged, en capabele, doch onconventionele healer. Hij is ten alle tijden beleefd en behulpzaam. Maar hij maakt en dult geen grapjes over The Balance, ook niet na verder finetunen van engineers. In combat opereert hij primair als defender en healer (hij is meer cleric dan Torden ooit zal zijn), en is best flexibel wanneer daar op enig moment geen behoefte aan is. Je leert ook dat als je iemand wilt arresteren, je dit beter expliciet kunt aangeven. Hemal blijkt anders schaamteloos voor de kill te gaan, waar hij energie uit lijkt te halen.

Mechanisch gezien is Hemal een Hexblade Warlock (lvl 12) en een Divine Soul Sorcerer (lvl 8). Die multiclass redundantie maakt hem potentieel underpowered in combat, dus mijn hoop is dat ik daadwerkelijk ook jullie zal kunnen gaan healen ipv alleen mezelf. Qua flavour en mechanics vind ik het gewoon zo gaaf dat ik het ga proberen.
