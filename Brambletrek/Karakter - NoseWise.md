## Reden voor mijn avontuur
Ik reis mee met mijn kompaan Yuzu om haar epische avonturen te verhalen, te dichten en te zingen
## My background
Growing up as a child of the forests' best tracker, you've learned the secrets of the forest and the art of survival. You can read the trails left by the animals, know which berries are safe to eat, and how to stay silent as a shadow. You've always been more at home among the trees than in the hustle bustle of the village.

## My trinket
A small crystal shard tied to a leather string, worn as a necklace. It's a piece of a larger crystal that your Seer ancestor found deep in the forest. The shard occasionally pulses with a soft glow, especially when in the proximiity to areas of strong magic.

## My legacy
I'm a story teller. I've loved the stories of my dad, and now I'm traveling with my older sister () to witness her adventures and re-tell her epic tales.
### Powers
- **Inspiring Tale**: once per day, during combat, apply -3 to all damage an opponent would deal for 2 turns
- **Song of Rest**: Once per day, restore 2 health and 2 morale to all who hear the song
- **Distract**: Once per day, during combat, confuse the opponent with a story, skip the opponent's next turn
- **Ageless Fables**: Once per day, use your knowledge to redraw an unfavourable event card.
