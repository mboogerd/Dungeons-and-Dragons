Haruto Takasawa is [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]'s biological father. He's also a self-made sailor and merchant who built his fleet of trade ships through sheer determination and strategic ambition. Often found working tirelessly on the docks or traveling to forge new trade routes, Haruto is a man of grit and charm. To Fukumi, he was a source of warmth and inspiration, full of adventurous tales—embellished but captivating—that painted the sea as a place of endless opportunity. Despite his frequent absences, Haruto’s stories and rough kindness made him a beloved figure in her childhood.
