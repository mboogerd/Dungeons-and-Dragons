Naran Baatar was [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]'s stepmother. She was also a fearless yokai hunter and the heart of the Baatar tribe’s defense against spiritual threats. Her sharp instincts and unyielding determination made her a respected protector and an admired figure among her people. As Fukumi’s adoptive mother, Naran not only trained her in combat and yokai hunting but also became her greatest role model. Her tragic death during a cave collapse, caused by a vengeful yokai, left a lasting impact on the tribe and deeply shaped Fukumi’s resolve to carry on her mother’s legacy.
