Altan Baatar is [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]'s stepfather. He is also the wise and compassionate leader of the Baatar tribe, balancing their nomadic traditions with feudal obligations to the Emperor. Known for his warmth and fairness, he settles disputes and guides the tribe’s migrations with an even hand. Altan’s wisdom and gentle discipline tempered Fukumi’s impulsive nature, shaping her into a thoughtful and strategic leader. He is revered for his ability to protect the tribe’s way of life while navigating the complexities of their responsibilities.
