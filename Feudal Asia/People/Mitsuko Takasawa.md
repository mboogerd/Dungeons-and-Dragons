Mitsuko Takasawa is [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]'s biological mother. She is also a shrewd and disciplined matriarch who meticulously manages the Takasawa family’s affairs to maintain and advance their position in Kinkou’s trade hierarchy. Her relentless drive for success shaped her relationship with Fukumi into one of cold discipline, emphasizing obedience and rigorous study. Mitsuko’s schemes, including arranging Fukumi’s marriage for political gain, created a rigid and oppressive environment, ultimately pushing Fukumi to escape the life her mother sought to impose.
