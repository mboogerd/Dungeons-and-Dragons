# Formations

## Open environments
### Formation: Sword
- Point: [[Ji Jon Wo|Ji]]
- Left/Right Guard: [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]
- Right/Left Guard: [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]
- Pommel: [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]]

This is the most offensive formation. Everyone is effectively on attack mode, with [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]] and [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]] also defending [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]]. Defense can be more easily compromised in this state, because [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]] and [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]] are not maximally separated from each other in this shape. This means we either have to give up [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]'s aura, or risk a wider array of AoE attacks that could target all three of us.
### Formation: Spear
We form one line, from front to back.
- Blade: [[Ji Jon Wo|Ji]]: Between enemies / 40+ ft. from [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]
- Shaft
	- Socket: [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]: 30 ft. in front of [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]
	- Center: [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]: center
	- Foot: [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]]: 30 ft. behind [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]

This formation balances offensive and defensive strength. [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]] and [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]] are maximally separated (while in [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]'s aura), which means that only 30+ft. AoE spells can affect the Shaft. This is less strong offensively than the Sword, and [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]'s role is limited to buff/healing, and catching stray attackers that have managed to pass both [[Ji Jon Wo|Ji]] and [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]].
### Formation: Trident
This formation is specifically made to put [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]] in the position to maximize damage output
- Three teeth
	- Left front: [[Ji Jon Wo|Ji]]: Use flurry of blows to Push enemies on the left towards the center (right)
	- Center back: [[Yuina Amano|Yuina]]: Optionally (rare resource): Employ compulsion to drive back advanced enemies into a cluster
	- Right front: [[Fukumi Baatar|Fukumi]]: Use Tactical Master on [[Yūki no Kaze no Kami|Yūki]] to Push enemies on the right towards the center (left)
- Shaft: [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]]: Strikes in the center of the enemy cluster to maximize AoE damage.
	- Conjure Volley
	- Steel Wind Strike
	- Lightning Arrow
	- Conjure Barrage
### Formation: Onion
This extreme formation is required when we are stormed from all sides. This isn't meant as a long-term position, the goal would be to find a way to give ourselves a back that is not under attack.
