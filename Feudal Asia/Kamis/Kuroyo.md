- Title: the Whispering Gale
- An ethereal dragon Kami of twilight and winds

The Kami that now 'inhabits' [[Kurai Tora|Kurai]]'s Long Bow Aeryn. Kuroyo is a symbol of subtlety, balance, and quiet strength—an ever-present yet intangible force, much like the wind itself. Its appearance blends beauty with an underlying sense of awe, inspiring both trust and reverence in those fortunate enough to perceive it.
