# Aorihan
A fox spirit and Kami of the Land who sometimes fancies themselves an aspect of the Kami of Balance and Prosperity

![[Aorihan.jpeg]]

# Backstory
Aorihan was once Eito Aorihara, a celebrated trader, diplomat, and devout follower of the Kami of Balance and Prosperity. In life, Aorihara journeyed across the lands of Futakazan, brokering peace treaties, establishing prosperous trade routes, and navigating treacherous disputes between warring clans. Known for their sharp wit, even temper, and unshakable integrity, Aorihara became a symbol of harmony in a world often torn by greed and conflict.
Their iconic gloves, worn through countless meetings and hardships, became a part of their legend. These gloves bore the marks of handshakes, trade deals, and even battles, symbolizing the enduring balance between peril and prosperity. Upon their death, Aorihara’s spirit naturally imbued the gloves, transforming them into a conduit for their Kami essence.
## The Gloves of Forceful Accord
The gloves are crafted from soft, durable leather, worn smooth from years of use. They are embroidered with intricate golden patterns that shimmer faintly in the light, symbolizing prosperity, and a dark-blue threadwork depicting scales, representing balance. When activated, the gloves radiate a soft, golden glow and faint wisps of ethereal mist rise from their seams, hinting at their Kami inhabitant.
## Guardian Form
When summoned, Aorihan appears as a three-tailed fox, symbolizing their cunning and connection to trade and balance. Their fur shimmers with hues of gold and deep blue, resembling the intricate embroidery of their gloves. Their eyes glow with a soft light, exuding wisdom and authority. Three tails flow behind them like elegant banners, each tipped with faintly glowing runes representing balance, prosperity, and perseverance. When they move, they walk on two legs, their paws leaving faint impressions resembling trade sigils or peace accords, which vanish after a few moments. In combat, they fly up and use their Bow of Radiant Balance, sowing devastation among their enemies.
