## Campaign Summary
- Matthew serves as one of CSI's earliest serious strategic contacts in [[Waterdeep]], translating their victories into legitimacy, direction, and institutional support.
- In Season 4 he helps frame the rise of [[Neonetheril]] as a geopolitical and magical threat rather than a string of isolated incidents.
- As the campaign grows more complex, Matthew becomes increasingly important as an organizer: assigning missions, connecting CSI to resources, coordinating agents, and keeping the wider picture visible.
- By Season 5 he is part of the shift from direct adventuring toward a hybrid structure of live missions plus agent deployment.
- In Seasons 6 and 7 he functions as one of the main operational anchors back in [[Waterdeep]], routing intelligence, passing on reports, and enabling urgent responses such as the rush to [[Cormyr]].
- He is rarely on the front line, but many of CSI's largest operations only happen because Matthew has already laid the groundwork.

## Current Themes
- Coordination over heroics.
- Building systems that let others act effectively.
- Managing a widening war without losing strategic coherence.
- Quiet authority backed by logistics, trust, and information.

## Role
Matthew is CSI's chief coordinator and intelligence organizer in Waterdeep, the person most responsible for turning the party's actions into a sustained campaign effort.
