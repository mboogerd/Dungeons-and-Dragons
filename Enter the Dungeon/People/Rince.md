- Rince Vannesser
- mage specialized in interdimensional magic and magical history
- Agent leading the [[ERK Librarians]] squads

## Campaign Summary
- Rince enters the campaign as a scholar-mage of interdimensional magic and magical history, but quickly proves that he is far more field-capable than a library stereotype suggests.
- From Season 4 onward he helps frame the larger threat of [[Neonetheril]], old magical cities, and the practical importance of artifacts such as the [[Sluitsteen]] and [[Chardalyn]].
- In Season 5 he becomes central to CSI's research arm through the [[ERK Librarians]], artifact analysis, magical detection work, and broader strategic planning.
- In Season 6 he helps redirect the campaign toward the [[Reghed gletsjer]], the Caves of Hunger, and ultimately [[Ythryn]], where his historical and arcane knowledge is consistently useful in interpreting Netherese remnants.
- At the end of Season 6 and start of Season 7, Rince's attempt to help [[Alma]] in [[Cormyr]] spirals into disaster, leading first to his capture and then to CSI's train rescue operation.
- After being recovered, he remains one of the most effective magical assets in the Suzail arc, despite clear trauma from imprisonment and torture.

## Current Themes
- Scholarship versus field danger.
- Curiosity and knowledge under wartime pressure.
- Interplanar research as a strategic necessity rather than a purely academic pursuit.
- Trauma, resilience, and loyalty after the Cormyr imprisonment.

## Role
Rince is CSI's principal magical historian and interdimensional specialist, combining deep theory with surprisingly effective field magic.
