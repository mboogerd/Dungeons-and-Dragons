Rurik is [[Torden]]'s father and husband of [[Sannl]]. He is somewhat of an innovator, finding ways to grow crops on the relatively fertile (due to volcanic ashes) and shielded ground (due to the descent relative to the surrounding barren planes) of [[Dwarven Valley]]. He's a simple man who embraces their harsh environment and rejoices in seeing life sprout from his efforts.

He eventually specializes in not just building simple staples, but in particular the herbs and flowers that [[Sannl]] needs a steady supply of for her brewery. 
# Character
Rurik will sometimes attend [[Sannl]]'s pub for a merry time, but generally prefers to be tending his sprouts.

As a father, he tries to share his passion with [[Torden]], who is not so receptive to "the whole plant story", and who side-eyes the noble craft of weapon smith. Rurik generally deals with that by finding new plants to cultivate, hoping that the novelty at some point will illuminate the beauty of plants to [[Torden]]. As a former shield dwarf, he hopes he can keep [[Torden]] away from all the fighting.

Rurik technically has a few employees who help him farm. But he hates to think of them this way. He puts in the work, and expects others to do the same. His attitude, matching that of most dwarfs, ensure that work just gets done. Rurik doesn't have a lot of respect for elves, who he finds too sensitive and fickle, duergar and drow, who he has some quarrels with, nor humans; who he finds deceptive. Rurik likes dwarfs, plants, and loves his family. That's enough for him.
