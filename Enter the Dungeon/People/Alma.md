## Campaign Summary
- Alma grows from early Gray Hand contact into one of CSI's most important strategic allies: a field spymaster focused on infiltration, informant networks, and quiet leverage.
- By Season 5 she is no longer just support staff, but part of the campaign's wider agent structure, operating through [[The Silent Blades]] and other off-screen intelligence work.
- Her work in [[Cormyr]] becomes one of the main engines of Season 7: she identifies the growing overlap between the [[Koninklijke crypte]], the [[Oud Heiligdom]], the [[Ambassade van Neonetheril]], and the strange succession around [[Alidane Thorne-Obarskyr]].
- Alma's report from [[Suzail]] is one of the clearest pieces of evidence that [[Zoros]] worship, political change, and [[Neonetheril|Neonetherese]] influence are converging.
- She is captured during the embassy operation with [[Rince]], which turns her from distant operator into the direct focus of a rescue arc.
- By the current campaign state she has been recovered, but much of the Cormyr conspiracy still rests on the lines of inquiry she uncovered.

## Current Themes
- Intelligence work as patient, dangerous, and politically decisive.
- Professional control under extreme pressure.
- The cost of operating behind enemy lines.
- Turning scattered clues into strategic direction for CSI.

## Role
Alma is CSI's most important field spymaster and intelligence-builder, especially in operations that depend on infiltration, local assets, and political pattern recognition.
