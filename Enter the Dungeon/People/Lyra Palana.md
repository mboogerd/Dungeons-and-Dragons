- Achtergrond: Lyra is getuige geweest van een overval van Drow. Dat dat gebeurde in haar gepriviligeerde omgeving, was een enorme schok. Het incident werd opgelost door warlocks, wat ze heel indrukwekkend vond. 20 jaar daarna kwamen warlocks scouten, en daar is Lyra in meegegaan, om de mooie wereld in stand te houden.
- Beschermers: [[Pyra]] & [[Tanny]]

## Campaign Summary
- Lyra starts from a privileged [[Silverymoon]] background, but the Drow raid she witnessed pushed her toward warlock power and a wider responsibility to protect the world.
- In Season 5 she becomes the party's clearest bridge to the [[Feywild]], especially through [[Ariana]], [[Paliset Hall]], and the journey to [[Harmony's heart]].
- She plays a central role in the [[Sluitsteen]] and keystone arc, helping secure Feywild-aligned power and carry it back into the wider campaign.
- In Season 6 the campaign becomes personal when [[Alais Palana]] is kidnapped by [[Feldressyx]], forcing Lyra into a rescue mission that ties family stakes directly to the war.
- Across Ythryn and Cormyr, Lyra keeps evolving into one of CSI's most capable infiltrators: elegant, socially precise, and very dangerous when subtlety fails.
- By Season 7 she is operating inside [[Suzail]] as one of the party's strongest assets for disguise, reconnaissance, and controlled violence.

## Current Themes
- Family ties versus the pull of the [[Feywild]].
- Protection of beauty and stability versus the brutal necessities of war.
- High social grace paired with increasingly ruthless effectiveness.
- A growing sense that her future may not lie entirely on the Material Plane.

## Party Role
Lyra is one of CSI's strongest infiltrators and social operators, while also serving as a major mythic link to the Feywild and the Sluitsteen arc.
