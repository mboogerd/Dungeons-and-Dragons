## Campaign Summary
- Ariana begins as Lyra's Feywild patron, but quickly proves to be much more than a distant benefactor: she is one of the campaign's key mythic and strategic powers.
- In Season 5 she becomes central through [[Harmony's heart]], where she receives CSI formally, stabilizes the shard crisis, and reframes the [[Sluitsteen]] as part of a larger anchoring system between planes.
- She identifies [[Zvoraxian the Primordial|Zvoraxian]] as a major enemy influence and treats the fight against him as both magical and civilizational.
- Ariana equips the party with gifts, magitech support, and a keystone mission, sending them to secure the [[Oude Klokketoren]] and strengthen the Material Plane's defenses.
- Her Ball of the Seasons marks an important transition in the campaign, publicly honoring CSI and helping move them from adventurers into trusted strategic actors.
- In Season 6 her older ties to figures like [[Hemal Void]] give more historical depth to her role, showing that she has been part of interplanar crises and difficult long-term decisions for far longer than CSI first realized.

## Current Themes
- Protection through culture, infrastructure, and long planning rather than brute force alone.
- The defense of the [[Feywild]] and the Material Plane as linked problems.
- Leadership that combines beauty, ritual, magitech, and real strategic hardness.
- Lyra's patronage as both personal bond and geopolitical alliance.

## Role
Ariana is one of CSI's most powerful allies: an archfey ruler, strategic patron, and major architect of the campaign's planar defense against Zvoraxian and related threats.
