Sannl is [[Torden]]'s mother and wife of [[Rurik]]. She is the master brewer in a small family brewery that descends for generations. It was never the biggest or the most famous in [[Dwarven Valley]] or the wider region around, but is gaining traction under Sannl's mastery. She is creative with alternative base supplies when logistics are hampered by winter storms. And she relies on alternative ingredients to flavor her creations, like local flowers and herbs. [[Rurik]] aids her by cultivating the crops that she needs in particular, ensuring that Sannl can deliver those beers with quality and consistency.

She likes to bring [[Torden]] along for him to help out in the brewery. [[Torden]] doesn't a remarkable talent for it, but he prefers the atmosphere and the work over what [[Rurik]] will have him do at the farms. He picks up quite a bit of the craft from her over time.
# Character
Sannl is mild-mannered and soft-spoken. As owner of the brewery, she is patient with both her employees and the clientele at the small accompanying pub. The occasionally rowdy Dwarf is quickly returning to his senses by a light touch of her hand and her disarming smile. And if some get too drunk, others ensure that the nuisance is dealt with so that her bar doesn't suffer damage.

As a mother, she is similar, patient and compassionate, though sometimes a bit absent. When not working at the brewery, she loves to go into the harsh environment, and explore nature in search of new ingredients. That those explorations are almost never fruitful doesn't demotivate her in the slightest; she then just experiments in the small to create new flavors with what she has.
