## Campaign Summary
- Gadget, also known as [[Kashet]], begins from exile: a kobold who fled the [[Coldbrand Horde]] and had to rebuild himself away from his old tribe.
- Across the campaign he becomes one of CSI's main technical minds, constantly translating strange systems, magitech, locks, vehicles, constructs, and arcane infrastructure into actionable knowledge.
- Season 5 sharpens that role through the [[Feywild]], [[Mechanica]], the creation of [[Sprocket]], and his growing comfort as an inventor rather than just a survivor.
- He repeatedly proves essential in high-pressure operations: solving mechanical problems, disabling magical systems, identifying enemy tech, and enabling infiltration plans.
- In Season 6 his history with draconic power and the Coldbrand world returns through [[Feldressyx]], the Frozen Forge, and the rescue of [[Alais Palana]], giving his background much more direct narrative weight.
- By Season 7 he is a key reason CSI can function against [[Neonetheril]] at all, especially when the problem is magitech, transport, surveillance, or enemy systems.

## Current Themes
- Proving his value, especially in relation to other knowledge-specialists like [[Rince]].
- Reclaiming draconic identity on his own terms rather than through domination.
- Curiosity as strength, but also as temptation.
- Turning enemy magitech into usable advantage before it turns the other way around.

## Party Role
Gadget is CSI's arcane engineer and systems specialist, the party member most likely to understand, break, repurpose, or outthink hostile magical technology.
