## Campaign Summary
- Zeno's arc begins with charisma and improvisation, but quickly gains deeper emotional weight through reconnection with the Derobledo family in and around [[Neverwinter]].
- Season 4 gives him one of the campaign's clearest belonging-questions: where he comes from, who his family is, and what place he wants in the world.
- In the middle seasons he grows into CSI's most dependable social operator, repeatedly solving problems through deception, timing, negotiation, and command presence.
- He also becomes increasingly important in the campaign's resonance and frequency threads, especially around magical systems such as the [[Sluitsteen]] and the [[Mythal]].
- In Ythryn, contact with [[Vlagomir]]'s spear changes him physically, pushing him toward a giant-like transformation and making his story more mythic and bodily at the same time.
- By Season 7 he is one of the party's main espionage drivers in [[Suzail]], helping turn CSI from survivors into active operators inside enemy territory.

## Current Themes
- Belonging, family expectation, and the fear of what he may find if he looks too directly at his roots.
- Control versus transformation as his body keeps changing.
- Charm and leadership backed by real strategic weight.
- Harmony, rhythm, and resonance as both magical tools and personal metaphors.

## Party Role
Zeno is CSI's principal face, improviser, and social field commander, often turning impossible situations survivable through nerve and creativity.
