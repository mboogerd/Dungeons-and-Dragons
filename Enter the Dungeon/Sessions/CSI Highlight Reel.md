# CSI Highlight Reel

A chronological shortlist of CSI's biggest set pieces, victories, and cinematic turning points.

## Season 4

### The Glacier Clash: Ardyr, Edradius, and Zuster Eva
- Session: [[S04E06]]
- Why it stands out: this is one of the first moments where CSI collides head-on with the larger Neonetheril conflict instead of just hearing about it from others.
- Highlights:
  - [[Ardyr]] reveals himself as a white dragon and becomes the center of a brutal field battle.
  - [[Zuster Eva]] lays out Neonetheril's justification and refuses to back down over the [[Chardalyn]].
  - The fight turns the campaign from regional adventuring into a continent-scale conflict.

## Season 5

### Paliset Hall and the Solitaire Shard
- Sessions: [[S05E04]], [[S05E05]]
- Why it stands out: CSI walks into a high Feywild intrigue scenario and discovers that the threatened relic is far more dangerous than it first appeared.
- Highlights:
  - [[Zorhanne Adulare]] has been replaced by a simulacrum.
  - The [[Solitaire Shard]] is revealed as a piece of [[Chardalyn]] tied to a rift and [[Far Realm]] corruption.
  - CSI rescues the real Zorhanne and contains the situation before it spirals further.

### Reaching [[Harmony's heart]]
- Sessions: [[S05E06]], [[S05E07]]
- Why it stands out: this is one of the campaign's strongest awe moments, where CSI is formally received into a much larger mythic and planar story.
- Highlights:
  - The party completes the [[Pad van de pelgrim]] and reaches [[Harmony's heart]].
  - [[Ariana]] receives CSI formally.
  - The [[Sluitsteen]] is reframed as part of a larger anchoring system between planes.

### The Clocktower Fight and the First Keystone Anchor
- Sessions: [[S05E08]], [[S05E09]]
- Why it stands out: this is both a major boss encounter and one of CSI's clearest world-changing accomplishments.
- Highlights:
  - [[Esadora Crane]] twists the old clocktower into a hostile, unstable battlefield of illusions, machinery, and poison.
  - CSI defeats Esadora after a long, hard fight.
  - The party restores the site and installs a keystone anchor, linking a Feywild domain to the Material Plane.

### Ariana's Ball and the Strategic Shift
- Session: [[S05E10]]
- Why it stands out: less a battle than a coronation moment for CSI's rise in stature.
- Highlights:
  - At the Ball of the Seasons, [[Ariana]] publicly honors the party.
  - CSI is no longer just a field group, but a trusted strategic actor.
  - The campaign pivots toward alliance-building, agents, anchors, and larger-scale resistance.

## Season 6

### Silverymoon Under Attack and the High Palace Defense
- Sessions: [[S06E01]], [[S06E02]]
- Why it stands out: this is one of CSI's biggest war-scale action sequences.
- Highlights:
  - [[Silverymoon]] tips from crisis into open assault.
  - CSI fights through the city toward the High Palace while the defense collapses around them.
  - [[Alustriël Silverhand]] and [[Taern Hornblade]] are saved at a critical moment.

### The Kidnapping of [[Alais Palana]]
- Session: [[S06E02]]
- Why it stands out: this is the emotional gut-punch that turns the Silverymoon arc into something personal.
- Highlights:
  - In the middle of the wider crisis, [[Feldressyx]] takes Lyra's mother.
  - The victory in the city immediately becomes a rescue mission.
  - The season's scale stays huge, but the stakes suddenly become intimate and brutal.

### The Frozen Forge and the Death of Feldressyx
- Sessions: [[S06E03]], [[S06E04]]
- Why it stands out: one of CSI's most cinematic and hard-won boss victories.
- Highlights:
  - The approach to Feldressyx's lair involves infiltration, traps, kobold warrens, and captive rescue.
  - The final fight plays out over ice, fire, magma, fear effects, and collapsing momentum.
  - CSI rescues [[Alais Palana]], survives the set piece, and kills [[Feldressyx]].

### Hemal Void's Final Moment
- Sessions: [[S06E06]], [[S06E07]]
- Why it stands out: one of the campaign's strongest emotional and mythic lore scenes.
- Highlights:
  - CSI encounters [[Hemal Void]], a tragic remnant of an older age.
  - His final exchange ties [[Ariana]], portal closure, and ancient interplanar history together.
  - The party leaves with knowledge, magical charms, and the sense of standing in the ruins of a much older catastrophe.

### The Fall of Iriolarthas
- Sessions: [[S06E08]], [[S06E09]]
- Why it stands out: this is arguably CSI's most mythic battle so far.
- Highlights:
  - [[Iriolarthas]] is confronted in the heart of fallen [[Ythryn]].
  - The fight is brutal and nearly fatal, with ash storms, necrotic devastation, and multiple party members dropping.
  - Victory unlocks major revelations about the [[Ythryn mythallar]], the obelisk, and the city's deeper magical core.

## Season 7

### Hijacking the Neonetheril Airship
- Session: [[S07E01]]
- Why it stands out: a perfect CSI operator moment, built on deception, nerve, and opportunism rather than brute force.
- Highlights:
  - CSI targets a [[Neonetheril]] airship near the [[Ardeep forest]].
  - The party uses impersonation and staged authority to seize the ship.
  - CSI turns enemy transport and magitech into its own strategic advantage.

### The Train Heist and Rescue of [[Rince]]
- Session: [[S07E02]]
- Why it stands out: one of the campaign's best heist-action hybrids.
- Highlights:
  - CSI scouts the train layout, Modrons, prison wagon, and timing in detail.
  - The heist turns into a live fight involving cult-aligned guards, an assassin, a black knight, and summoned elementals.
  - The party frees [[Rince]] and escapes with [[Word of Recall]].

### The Embassy Infiltration and Rescue of [[Alma]]
- Session: [[S07E03]]
- Why it stands out: a pressure-cooker espionage mission that pays off the whole opening of the Cormyr arc.
- Highlights:
  - CSI infiltrates the [[Ambassade van Neonetheril]] under cover and finds [[Alma]] below ground.
  - The escape exposes the real threat posed by [[Arkady Mirov Karsin]] and [[Serafim]].
  - The party gets Alma out alive and escapes before the embassy can close around them.

### The Crypte Raid and Running Battle with the Embassy Kill Team
- Session: [[S07E04]]
- Why it stands out: one of the most desperate and dangerous operations in the current arc.
- Highlights:
  - CSI sabotages the new airport, then pushes into the [[Koninklijke crypte]].
  - The crypt exploration turns into an ambush by embassy-linked killers, with [[Serafim]] pressing the attack.
  - The party only survives by breaking contact and extracting under extreme pressure.
