# Sessions

This folder contains the session-by-session campaign notes for **Enter the Dungeon**, organized by season.

## Campaign Overview

At a high level, the campaign follows CSI as it grows from a capable adventuring party into a major force operating across cities, planes, and political systems. The long-running backbone of the story is the conflict around [[Neonetheril]], the [[Sluitsteen]], [[Chardalyn]], and the wider struggle over ancient magical infrastructure, planar stability, and destructive power tied to [[Zoros]], [[Talos]], and related forces.

Across the seasons, the campaign gradually shifts in scale:
- from local heroics and regional expeditions
- to strategic artifact recovery and alliance-building
- to city-scale crises and mythic ruins
- to active espionage and political conflict inside [[Cormyr]]

## Seasons
- [[Season 4]]: Waterdeep, Icewind Dale, early Neonetheril conflict, Chardalyn, and the first major Zoros and glacier threads.
- [[Season 5]]: ERK conspiracies, Feywild relics, Ariana's anchoring project, and the opening of the Silverymoon crisis.
- [[Season 6]]: Silverymoon under attack, the Feldressyx rescue arc, and the Ythryn expedition.
- [[Season 7]]: the in-progress Cormyr and Suzail espionage arc, focused on rescuing Alma and Rince and uncovering the wider conspiracy.

## Through-Lines
- [[Neonetheril]] as the central geopolitical and magical threat
- The [[Sluitsteen]] and related anchoring or portal infrastructure
- [[Chardalyn]] as both resource and danger
- [[Zoros]], [[Talos]], and destructive power as recurring spiritual and ideological pressure
- CSI's growth from adventurers into organizers, agents, and strategic actors

## Current Status

As of the latest season notes, the campaign is in the middle of the [[Cormyr]] arc. [[Season 7]] is still in progress.
