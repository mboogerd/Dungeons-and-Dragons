# Pre-session
## Goal
Stop Neonetheril and their nefarious yet nebulous plans, by:
- Collecting intelligence
- Sabotaging plans
- Building a coalition of different Factions (Nations, Tribes, Organizations)
- Anything else that makes sense to you :)
- And finally: confront Neonetheril in The Ultimate Battle. And note: what that battle looks like is up to you and your choices (debate club or dance-off, anyone?)
## Means
- Perform missions yourselves (sources: Ariana, Laeral Silverhand, Matthew Brandt) [live play]
- Assign Agents and Squads to missions [downtime]
- Perform your own downtime activities, from outfitting your castle to inventing magitech to spreading propaganda [downtime]
- Your allies may provide Agents or Squads for you to use during downtime, depending on your influence with them. You can gain influence by all the means mentioned above, although typically it will involve at least one live play mission.
- Squads are a group of regular NPCs, but they still are professionals trained for specific goals, this may range from reconnaissance to scientific research to straight up large-scale battles. 
- Agents are your own cadre of heroes, they are outstanding experts who can lead Squads and increase their success chance or perform special missions of their own (of course CSI remain the **superheroes** :-)
- Currently, you have the following Agents and Squads available:
- From Luskan, as a group you can choose one of:
	- Agent: Detective Inspector Simmons. Street smart, specialized in interrogation and piecing together evidence on the fly. Supported by the "Blood Hounds" Squad of detectives, skills: surveillance, investigation, underworld infiltration
	- Agent: Newt "Gearhead", specialized in experimental magitech. Supported by Research Squad "Skunkworks", skills: investigation, experimentation, engineering
- From Waterdeep:
	- Agent: [[Alma]], spy master specialized in infiltration and setting up informant networks; Squad: Grey Hand Squad "[[The Silent Blades]]". Skills: infiltration, intelligence-gathering, sabotage, assassination
	- Agent: [[Rince]], mage specialized in interdimensional magic and magical history; Research Squad: [[ERK Librarians]]. Skills: research, history, investigation
	- Marine Squad "[[The Iron Tide]]" currently manning CSI castle defense. Specialized in amphibious assault, setting up defenses and fortifications. Skills: combat, engineering
- When you as a group decide to play some downtime, you can assign Agents and Squads a mission and an amount of time to complete these mission (months). As a DM, I will resolve these missions in between sessions (live if possible). Missions can be successful and lead to knowledge, influence, or assets. They can also lead to (permanent) injury or death of Squad members and Agents, and are never risk-free!

Patroon van aanvallen? Er zit een soort van wanhoop of bewijsdrang achter de overvallen die normaal sporadisch voorkomen, Het zijn vluchtelingen, jongeren die zichzelf moeten bewijzen, ze nemen risico om iets groots te bemachtigen, kleine losstaande groepjes, en dan verdwijnen ze ook - als ze iemand hebben gekidnapt, of magisch artifact hebben gevonden. Deze groepen blijven terugkomen maar vluchten op het moment dat de wacht achter ze aan hebben gestuurd en lijken vrij risicomijdend. Dat maakt het patroon anders dan de kleine groepjes wanhopige of risicozoekende overvallers die we normaal zien. Hier meer sprake van militaire campagne dan willekeurige overvallen - blijkt uit hoeveelheden en manier waarop de overvallen worden gedaan. 

Een groter leger met verzwakte verdedigings-linies, stad kan overvallen worden, er zijn hier bijzondere artefacten en kennis aanwezig, en we hebben de mensen die hier wonen, we moeten dit uitermate serieus nemen.
