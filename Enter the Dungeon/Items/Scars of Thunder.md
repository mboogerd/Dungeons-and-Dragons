Scars of Thunder (Rare, requires attunement by a Cleric of Talos)

These bracelets are adorned with intricate lightning motifs and emanate an electric charge. When attuned, it amplifies your connection to both Talos and Zvoraxian, allowing you to channel their powers in devastating ways.

## Effect 1: Wrath of Talos
- **Description**: Channeling Talos’ fury, you can unleash a thunderous blast.
- **Mechanics**: Once per long rest, as an action, you can cast Thunderwave at 4th level without expending a spell slot. When you do, you deal an additional 1d10 thunder damage.
- **Price**: After using this ability, you have disadvantage on your next saving throw against being frightened or charmed. Additionally, your appearance shifts slightly, with the scars on your skin sparking with energy, and you may gain a slight aura of static electricity.
# Effect 2: Hope of Talos
- **Description**: By invoking Talos' cleansing might, you can augment your healing power.
- **Mechanics**: Once per long rest, as part of a healing spell, you can remove one adverse effect on each creature you heal.
- **Price**: after using this ability, you take on the burden of the creature you heal, sacrificing some of your magical might. For 1 minute, for each effect you remove, you suffer a -1 on your spell attack rolls.
